# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Skipper to be a welcoming project for everyone. We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy toward other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive comments
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Any other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the maintainer at **jerryeagbesi@gmail.com**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.
